Food

Pike Place Market

Although Pike Place can feel overridden with tourists, there are some lesser-known spots that you should definitely hit. It's easy to spend a day exploring the market but it's also a great spot to stop for a quick bite before hitting the town.

Experience

5th Avenue Theater

Upcoming Shows / Directions

The historic 5th Avenue Theater, opened in 1926, is the most beautiful theater in Seattle. Only a few streets up from Pike Place, the only thing that makes a night at the 5th better is a gyro and red bean ball from the market beforehand. Click here to learn about Teentix, a program that allows anyone under 21 to purchase tickets at an extremely discounted price.

Golden Gardens

More Info

Hidden in the Ballard neighborhood, Golden Gardens is a beautiful beach perfect for picnics, volleyball games, and watching the sunset. Bring a blanket, your guitar, and some friends!
